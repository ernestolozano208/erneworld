The plastic bag is such a genius invention and the beauty of it has always passed me by. I got a new gig and I am seeing more plastic bags than usual and I couldn’t help but notice how useful they really are. They can carry a decent amount of object and if two are put together they can carry even more weight. Plastics bags are also self sufficient they can contain a huge quantity of themselves. With the right care and attention they can be used for many many trips. I did a little digging and originally the plastic bag was invented in response to how much worse paper and cotton bags were for the environment. Now the plastic bag is a major issue for our planet but it originally was intended for pure good. Really interesting stuff and do not get me started on the invention of plastic in general.
